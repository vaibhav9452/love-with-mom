Meri Pyari Mummy

Aap meri zindagi ki sabse badi khushi ho.

Aapka pyaar meri har jeet ka raaz hai.

Aapki god sabse sukoon bhari jagah hai.

Jab bhi mushkil aayi, aapne apne haathon se sambhala.

Aapke bina duniya adhoori lagti hai.

Main hamesha aapko khush dekhna chahta/chahti hoon.

Love you forever, Mummy!

Dil se... Thank You Mummy!
